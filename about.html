About Andrew

Andrew is currently a student at Michigan State University, studying for a bachelor's degree in the field of Information Science. In his pursits of education, Andrew also became a member of the Sigma Tau Gamma fraternity on campus, where his fraternity's philanthropy devotes itself to the special olympics. Growing up in Howell, Michigan, Andrew had always wanted to attend Michgian State to follow in his older brother's footsteps. After entering into college with his best friend, he knew that he found where he belonged on campus and in life. Andrew's ambitions focus on mainly international aspects, with Andrew planning a trip to Japan with his three best friends once they all graduate from college.

Other Aspects

Andrew is also a student at University of Michigan Flint, Where he studies for a bachelor's degree in Accounting. The main reason why Andrew decided to double major was that his mother is an accountant and would like to pursure her same field of work, while also having the skills that come with information science as well. Although he may not know what job he wants to pursue, Andrew knows one thing, he wants money!
